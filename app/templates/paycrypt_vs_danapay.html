Thinking in terms of risk and licenses

Betting operators often hold specific licenses (for example Curacao or other offshore frameworks) with explicit rules on reporting and AML.

  • Look for providers that offer exportable reports and transaction logs compatible with your auditors.
  • Separate your risk systems from the gateway so you can switch processors without rewriting core logic.
  • Prefer simple, auditable deposit flows over complex smart-contract UX.

PayCrypt leans into this by giving you a crypto cashier tailored to betting, while assuming you already have internal KYC, risk scores, and AML controls.

Combining providers in practice

In reality, many operators do not choose a single provider forever. Instead they combine tools:

  • One gateway for high‑volume USDT deposits and payouts.
  • Another PSP for cards, bank rails, or regional fiat methods.
  • Internal routing logic to steer players based on country, ticket size, or risk.
