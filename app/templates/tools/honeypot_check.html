How Honeypot Detection Works
What is a Honeypot?

A honeypot token allows users to buy but prevents them from selling, effectively trapping their funds.

Detection Methods:
  • Smart contract analysis
  • Buy/sell simulation
  • Tax structure examination
  • Trading restriction checks
Red Flags:
  • Extremely high sell taxes (>25%)
  • Cannot sell all tokens
  • Trading cooldowns
  • Hidden owner functions
  • Blacklist mechanisms
